# Bikrito-BD

**Expose Sexual Violence in Bangladesh | Public Archive & Awareness Platform**

Bikrito-BD is a public archive documenting sexual violence in Bangladesh. It collects authentic news with titles and links, allowing users to view, verify, and vote to fact-check. The platform raises awareness, empowers citizens, and amplifies victims’ voices.

## Mission
- Archive authentic news about sexual violence.  
- Enable users to verify facts and participate in voting.  
- Raise public awareness about justice and victim rights.  

## How to Contribute
1. Add news entries with title and link.  
2. Vote to verify authenticity of existing entries.  
3. Report any false or misleading information.  

> Together, we can raise awareness and demand justice for victims in Bangladesh.
